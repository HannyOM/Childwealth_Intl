About Us

Welcome to Childweath International School

Established in 2005, our rich tradition of academic excellence and moral integrity nurtured the minds of our students and shaped the future in a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of holistic education that empowers every child to excel academically, socially, and emotionally, preparing them for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

Providing quality education tailored to each student’s unique potential.

Fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Encouraging values of respect and responsibility to build well-rounded individuals.
